# Cod Dynasty Progress

Cod Dynasty is now a standalone game repo at `matty51299-a11y/Cod-Dynasty`.

This repo was forked/copied from CDL Manager 2026 (`matty51299-a11y/cod-league-manager`) as a working engine baseline. CDL Manager 2026 should not be affected by changes made in this standalone repo.

## Current Engine Baseline

The copied systems currently include:

- Match simulation
- CDL and Challenger rosters
- Contracts, budgets, transfers and buyouts
- Scouting and recruitment
- Morale and team dynamics
- Owner/board objectives
- Staff systems
- Inbox/news/event flow
- Player and team profiles/history
- Awards and season summaries
- Existing UI screens and navigation

The first goal is to preserve the working copied engine while renaming and separating the project as Cod Dynasty. Modern/CDL copied mode can remain temporarily as the stable baseline while historical systems are built.

Historical COD Dynasty work has not started yet. Do not import historical rosters, build Ghosts mode, or change core gameplay systems until a dedicated historical foundation task begins.

## Cod Dynasty Direction

Planned direction:

1. Historical COD eras database
2. Ghosts starting rosters
3. Season progression from Ghosts to Advanced Warfare
4. Yearly game/title transitions
5. Historical teams and players
6. Rookie/debut classes by COD title
7. 4v4 and 5v5 era support
8. Historical tournament formats later

## Current Status

- Project branding setup/rename pass is in progress.
- Current gameplay systems remain copied from CDL Manager 2026 intentionally.
- The long-term goal is Historical COD Dynasty mode starting in Call of Duty: Ghosts and progressing through COD titles season by season.
